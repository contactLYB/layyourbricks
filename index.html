How It Works

Community investing made simple — from your neighborhood to your portfolio.

Explore local businesses raising capital in your neighborhood. See their story, their plan, and exactly how your investment helps.

Why Lay Your Bricks?

Capital should circulate in communities, not just flow out of them.

Built for Everyday Investors

You don't need $100,000 or a financial advisor. Start with $50 and invest in what you know — the businesses you already support.

Hyperlocal by Design

This isn't a national algorithm. Lay Your Bricks starts in Baltimore — because the distance between investor and investment should be measured in blocks, not ticker symbols.

Real Returns, Real Impact

Community investing isn't charity. Target 5–10% returns while every dollar recirculates locally at 3–4x the rate of money spent at chains.

Transparent & Accountable

Every business is vetted. Every dollar is tracked. Built-in education, compliance tools, and incentives keep the ecosystem healthy.
